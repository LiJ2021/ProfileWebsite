About me

I have always been curious about how things work. At a young age I was often found taking things apart - radios, telephones, and old televisions, trying to discover what made them work. Along the way I started tinkering with computer hardware - trying to figure out how they stored and displayed information locally, and what components could be integrated to make them function differently. Exploring computer hardware seemed to naturally lead to computer software, and I have been exploring technology ever since.

These interests led me to pre-engineering programs in high school, and then to engineering in college. All learning did not take place on college campuses though - I have been able to complete different certification programs and bootcamps that led to obtaining my CompTIA A+, Microsoft Certified Professional, and Microsoft Certified Systems Administrator, as well as certifications in Web Development - utlizing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React.js, MongoDB and PostgreSQL.
